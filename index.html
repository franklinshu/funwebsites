About The Fun Button

In a world full of productivity hacks and endless to-do lists, we believe in the power of a good, old-fashioned, useless break. The Fun Button is your curated portal to the most delightfully pointless, wonderfully weird, and surprisingly fun corners of the internet. This project started as an answer to the simple question: "What happens when you press a button that promises nothing but a random smile?"

Our mission is to preserve the playful spirit of the web. We manually review every site to ensure it's safe, fun, and SFW (Safe For Work, mostly).

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This site uses browser storage to remember which sites you've "liked", but this information is stored only on your computer and is not sent to us. We use Google Analytics to understand our traffic, which collects anonymous data. For ad purposes, Google and its partners may use cookies. We do not store any personal data ourselves.
