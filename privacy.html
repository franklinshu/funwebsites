Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At The Fun Button, we believe that discovering fun websites should be worry-free. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website to explore the entertaining corners of the internet.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Website Submissions: When you submit a website through our "Submit a Site" form, we collect the URL and optional description you provide.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us, we may collect your email address and any information you choose to share in your message.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site, including pages visited, time spent, and general location (country/region level only).
  • Browser Storage: Your "liked" websites are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • To review and potentially add submitted websites to our collection
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To analyze website usage and improve our service
  • To comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our website (like hosting services)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition, etc.)

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Google Analytics: To understand website usage patterns
  • Advertising: Google and advertising partners may use cookies to show relevant ads
  • Functionality: To remember your preferences (stored locally in your browser)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

International Users

Our service is available worldwide. If you're accessing The Fun Button from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information
  • Objection to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
